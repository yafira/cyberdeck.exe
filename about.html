open call // SOFTER residency apps open

Is the future of computing homemade?

SOFTER is inviting creative technologists and curious makers to a two-week funded residency to design, build, and demystify cyberdecks.

In a world of increasingly black-boxed technologies and rapidly growing AI models, cyberdecks feel like a safe haven, a breath of fresh air, devices that give us hope for brighter tech futures built on playfulness, participation, and decentralisation.
